About Me

As a trilingual Speech-Language Pathologist, I have always been passionate about working with linguistically and culturally diverse patients. Growing up in an Arabic speaking household, I know and appreciate the vast language opportunities that accompany being bilingual.

My academic career started at Northwestern University where I completed my masters in Speech-Language Pathology. It was during this training that I learned how to best serve diverse patient populations.

I look forward to working with your family. We'll piece together unique intervention strategies that will best support your loved one's communication, just like a mosaic!

Expressive/Receptive Language Disorders

Language disorders consisting of difficulties with understanding or using spoken or written language.

Available in: English, Spanish

Phonological Disorders

Speech sound disorder that involves difficulties with a sound system, and use of incorrect speech patterns.

Available in: English, Spanish, Arabic

Childhood Apraxia of Speech (CAS)

CAS is a motor speech disorder which is characterized by difficulty planning and movements needed for speech.

Available in: English, Spanish, Arabic

Articulation Disorders

Articulation disorders consist of difficulties producing speech sounds correctly due to incorrect placement or problem with mouth, tongue, or lip movements.

Available in: English, Spanish, Arabic
